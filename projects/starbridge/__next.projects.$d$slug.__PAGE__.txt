StarBridge is a comprehensive AI-powered platform designed to support individuals with intellectual disabilities in their daily lives. The project aims to create bridges to better communication, independence, and quality of life through intelligent assistance.

## Vision

The platform leverages large language models to provide personalized support, adapting to each user's unique needs and communication style. It includes features for caregivers and family members to stay connected and informed about their loved ones' progress and well-being.

## Why This Matters

This project represents my belief that AI technology should be accessible to everyone and can be a powerful tool for social good when designed with empathy and understanding.

## Key Innovations

- **Adaptive Communication**: The AI learns and adapts to each user's unique communication patterns
- **Safety-First Design**: Built with robust safeguards to ensure appropriate and supportive interactions
- **Family Connection**: Caregivers stay informed while respecting user privacy and autonomy
