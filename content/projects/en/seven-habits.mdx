7Habits aims to turn the principles in The 7 Habits of Highly Effective People into a habit system that can actually be practiced, reviewed, and sustained.

## Direction

- Extract an actionable structure from the book
- Organize tracking and reflection on a weekly rhythm
- Let AI participate in reminders, review, and reinforcement
- Connect abstract principles to concrete actions and long-term change

## Why it matters

Many habit tools only solve for check-ins. The harder part is connecting habits back to values, goals, and reflection. That is where this project is focused.

## Current state

The project is still in the planning phase. The immediate goal is to produce a full PRD before moving into implementation.
