ArtFrame is a mobile-focused project where the main challenge is not business complexity, but whether the capture flow feels smooth, intuitive, and well finished.

## What it focuses on

- Whether the camera preview is visible as soon as the screen opens
- Whether bottom tab buttons are truly centered and aligned
- Whether the shutter button sits in the right place for one-handed use

## Why it matters

Projects like this are useful because they test a simple truth: product quality is often decided by camera screens, alignment, feedback, and transitions, not only by large features.

## Current state

The project is still iterating on the capture screen and bottom navigation, with the goal of making the core interaction feel natural enough to disappear.
