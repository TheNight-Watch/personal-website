AI Experiments is my playground for exploring new AI capabilities, testing ideas, and learning about emerging technologies. This collection includes various proof-of-concepts, demos, and experimental applications that may eventually grow into full projects.

## Areas of Exploration

- **Prompt Engineering**: Testing and refining prompts for various use cases
- **Multi-modal AI**: Experiments with vision, audio, and text models
- **Agent Systems**: Building autonomous AI agents for specific tasks
- **Novel Interfaces**: Exploring new ways for humans to interact with AI

## Why Experiment?

From testing new model capabilities to prototyping novel interaction patterns, these experiments help me stay at the forefront of AI development and inform my thinking about potential product opportunities.
