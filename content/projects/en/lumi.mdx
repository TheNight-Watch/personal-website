Lumi is an AI-powered children's drawing education platform that nurtures creativity through intelligent assistance and feedback. The project was born during my entrepreneurship venture in Shenzhen, where I received incubation funding from the Shenzhen Science and Innovation Academy.

## The Approach

The platform uses AI to provide real-time guidance and encouragement as children draw, adapting to their skill level and interests. Rather than replacing the creative process, Lumi aims to enhance it by providing supportive feedback that helps children develop their artistic abilities while maintaining the joy of creation.

## Lessons Learned

Although the startup venture faced challenges, the project taught me valuable lessons about:

- Building products for children
- Working with AI in educational contexts
- The importance of iterative user testing with young audiences

## Future Direction

We continue to develop Lumi with a focus on making art education more accessible and engaging for children everywhere.
